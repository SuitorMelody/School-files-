About

Elod's farm is concerned with animals that are raised for meat, fibre, egg or other products. It includes day-to-day care, management, production, nutrition, selective breeding, and the raising of livestock.

You can apply for how to start animal husbandary at home and learn it within some few weeks. You can also contact me for more details!
